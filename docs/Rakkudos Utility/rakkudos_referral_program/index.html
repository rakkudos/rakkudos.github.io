The Rakkudos Referral Program (RR)

The Rakkudos Referral (RR) program turns the Rakkudos community into a money making machine, both for the Shakudo product suite, and for the Rakkudos community. Let's review the key elements of the RR program:

How much do Rakkudos holders get paid for a referral?​

The most important question, of course, is how much commission can Rakkudos expect to get? We see the Rakkudos NFT as a partnership between Shakudo and the Web3 community, starting with Solana. As a result, we're launching the referral with a bang and setting eye-poppingly high referral fees for the launch. The referral fees are broken down by product line:

  • Shakudo Platform - the core platform provided by Shakudo that enables data and web3 teams to rapidly and self-sufficiently develop, scale, deploy and maintain their work. With the Shakudo Platform, teams can choose any cloud or data center for their underlying infrastructure, or they can let Shakudo choose the most optimal infrastructure for them. To celebrate the launch of Rakkudos, Shakudo is offering special pricing for the Shakudo Platform with a flat fee of $10,000 USD / year for under 10 users and $1,000/user/year for additional users. This pricing does not include infrastructure usage costs.

For each qualified sale of the Shakudo Platform through a referral, Shakudo will pay 60% of the first year platform fees to the Rakkudos treasury up to a maximum of $20,000 USD per referral. The Rakkudos treasury will then distribute the commission to the referrer and all other qualified Rakkudos holders. The commission will be denominated in SOL for the primary referrer and in preKUDOS to other Rakkudos holders.

  • Shakudo Managed RPC Nodes and Validators - the referral fee for validator and RPC customers is 10% of the first year contract to the Rakkudos treasury, which will then be distributed to the referrer and other qualified Rakkudos holders. For up to date RPC node and validator pricing, please visit shakudo.io.

The referral fees may be adjusted over time for a variety of business considerations.

What's the split between the referrer and the Rakkudos community?​

Rakkudos is one big giant family of happy raccoons that work together to create a brighter future. Unlike most (all?) referral programs, the RR program reward the community with a large commission for each referral. We've designed the program this way because we believe that the network effects of the community will be a more powerful mechanism for spreading the word than any individual's network.

For each referral payout, the referrer will receive 40% of the reward and the community will receive the remaining 60%. Here's where it gets really interesting: to reward long term community members we'll look at on-chain data and the duration of their current soft-stake period. Anyone who's been qualified for referral payouts and has over 100 days of current active stake will receive their full share of the referral payout. Rakkudos holders who's current active stake period is less than 100 days, will receive a prorated share. For example, if Alice has been staking her Rakkudo for 66 days she will receive 66% of the per-Rakkudo community payout.

The best part: Rakkudos are stackable for referral fees. Each Rakkudo earns its own equal share of the community payout, and holding multiple Rakkudos gives the holder the commission of all the Rakkudos that they have staked.

Mint note: in the first 100 days post mint, the staking requirements of RR will be waived and every qualified Rakkudos holder at the time of payout will receive their share.

What are the qualification criteria for participating in RR?​

The intent is to allow as many Rakkudos holders as possible to participate in the referral program. Due to regulatory constraints we may not be able to make payments to individuals in certain jurisdictions. The two rules for qualifying for an RR payout are:

  • Hold a Rakkudo in your wallet
  • Accept the terms and conditions of the RR program via the Rakkudos website (page to be set up shortly post mint)
